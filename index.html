Our Story

Legacy of Royal Kitchens

Saffron Palace was born from heirloom recipes gathered across Rajasthan, Kerala backwaters, and the spice markets of Hyderabad. Every plate celebrates mortar-and-pestle masalas and slow-tempered ghee.

  • Farm-to-Tandoor: Seasonal produce kissed by smokey clay ovens.
  • Spice Stewardship: Single-origin spices stone-ground in-house.
  • Cultural Authenticity Badges: Ministry of Tourism certified, Slow Food India advocate.
